About Us

Get to Know the Team Behind the Green.

With over five decades of expertise, the Vikas Group has been a trailblazer in transforming the real estate landscape of Mumbai and Khandala. Founded by Shri Madanlal Gupta in 1972, the group began its journey with Vikas Apartments in Juhu and has since delivered iconic projects that combine innovation, integrity, and sustainability.

In 2002, the EMGEE Group emerged as an offshoot of the Vikas Group, carrying forward the legacy while bringing a fresh perspective to luxury real estate. Known for its expertise in creating premium residential, commercial, and township projects, the EMGEE Group is synonymous with meticulous design, superior quality, and trust.

Connectivity at Emgee Greens

Classic Connectivity To New Lonavala

Strategically located in New Lonavala, EMGEE Greens enjoys seamless connectivity to Mumbai, Pune, and beyond. The upcoming Mumbai-Pune Missing Link expressway and Atal Setu have drastically reduced travel times, making it easier than ever to reach this tranquil retreat.

The upcoming Navi Mumbai Airport further enhances accessibility for domestic and international travel, making EMGEE Greens a coveted destination for second homes. With proximity to renowned attractions like Bhaja and Karla Caves, EMGEE Greens is perfectly positioned to combine modern living with natural beauty.
